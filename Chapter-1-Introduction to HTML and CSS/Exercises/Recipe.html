Homemade Pizza

With this recipe, enjoy creating delicious, cheesy, and fresh pizza at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 packet or 2 tsp of active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup of wark water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp of sugar
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (chicken, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives etc...)

Pizza Sauce Recipe

Make your own fresh and tasty pizza sauce at home.

  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar

How To Make Pizza Sauce Recipe

  1. Heat oil on the pan
  2. Add minced garlic and saute for 1 minute
  3. pour in crushed tomatoes and stir for 10 to 15 minutes
  4. Add salt, pepper and sugar
  5. Coock for 10 to 15 minutes
  6. Let it cool down before spreading it on pizza dough

Method

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it rise for 5 to 10 minutes util foamy
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and oil. Mix until a dough is forms
  3. Knead the dough on floured surface for about 5 to 8 minutes until smooth
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for 2 hours or until doubled in size
  5. Preheat the oven to 220 dgree celsius
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it into a pizza base of your desier thickness
  7. Transfer the dough on to the pizza tray
  8. Spread homemade pizza sauce on the base
  9. Sprinkle cheese and add the toppings
  10. Bake for 25 minutes until the curst is golden brown and the cheese is melted
  11. Remove from the oven and served hot
