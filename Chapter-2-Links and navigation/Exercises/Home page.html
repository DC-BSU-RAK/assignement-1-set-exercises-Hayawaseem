About The Movie

Yes Day is a popular family film where parents agree to say "yes" to nearly everything their kids ask for, within reason. The movie playfully reverses roles, giving kids power for a day to promote fun and family bonding.

Quick Facts

  • Release Date: March 12, 2021
  • Genre: Family / Comedy
  • Plot: A couple gives their kids a 24-hour "Yes Day" where the children make the rules (within reason).
  • Director: Miguel Arteta
  • Main Cast: Jennifer Garner, Edgar Ramirez, Jenna Ortega

Yes Day Common Rules

  • Set a budget limit
  • All activities must be safe and legal
  • Requests must be made in advance
  • Everyone must participate and have fun!
