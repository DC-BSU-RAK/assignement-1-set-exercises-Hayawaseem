About The Movie

Yes Day is a popular family film in which parent consent to say "yes" to nearly everything their kids ask for throught a full day, as long as they stay within reasonable and established bounds. Temporarily reversing the typical parent child roles is intended to give children greater freedom and power while promoting enjoyment and closer family bond.

Quick Facts

  • Release Date: March 12, 2021
  • Genre: Family / Comedy
  • Plot Summary: Tired of always saying "no," a couple decides to give their three kids a Yes Day for 24 hours where the kids make the rules (within limits).
  • Director: Miguel Arteta
  • Main Cast: Jennifer Garner, Edgar Ramirez, Jenna Ortega

Yes Day Common Rules

  • Set budget limit
  • All activities must be safe and legal
  • Requests must be made in advance (no last-minute surprises)
  • Everyone has to participate and have fun!
