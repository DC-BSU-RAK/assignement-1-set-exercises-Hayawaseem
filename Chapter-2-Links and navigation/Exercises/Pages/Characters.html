Plot

The family comedy "Yes Day" features parents who are famous for saying "no", giving their three children a day to set the rules. The family's wild journey and discovery of the value of time together leads to hilarious chaos

Characters

Allison Torres: The strict but loving mother.

Carlos Torres The fun-spirited father who balances work and family.

Katie Torres The rebellious teenager pushing her parents to loosen up and relax.
